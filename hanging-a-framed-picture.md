# How to Hang a Framed Picture

## What You'll Need

- Framed picture
- Nail or picture hook
- Hammer
- Tape measure
- Pencil
- Level
- Stud finder or cable detector

## Steps

1. **Choose your spot.** Hold the picture against the wall at eye level and decide where you want it.
2. **Mark the position.** Use a pencil to lightly mark the top centre of the frame on the wall.
3. **Measure the hanging point.** Flip the picture over and measure the distance from the top of the frame down to the hanging wire or bracket. Subtract this distance from your wall mark to find where the nail needs to go.
4. **Check for hidden cables.** Before drilling or hammering, scan the area around your nail mark with a cable detector or stud finder that has a live-wire detection mode. Move the detector slowly across the wall in a 15–20 cm radius around the mark. If the detector signals a live cable, choose a different spot and re-scan. Never drive a nail into a wall without confirming the area is clear.
5. **Mark the nail point.** Make a small pencil mark on the wall where the nail will be driven in.
6. **Drive the nail.** Hammer the nail or picture hook into the wall at a slight upward angle, leaving about 1 cm exposed.
7. **Hang the picture.** Lift the frame and hook the wire or bracket onto the nail.
8. **Level it.** Place a level along the top edge of the frame and adjust until it sits straight.
9. **Step back and check.** View the picture from a few paces back to make sure the placement looks right.
